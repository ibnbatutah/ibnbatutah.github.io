About

At OpenChain, we're preparing to launch an innovative supply chain solution powered by a DAG-based distributed ledger. Our upcoming system will offer unparalleled transparency and traceability, enabling you to track materials from their origin through every stage of their lifecycle, including recycling and reuse. By assigning each product a unique digital identity, our solution is designed to ensure that your resources are sustainably sourced, efficiently managed, and responsibly recycled, helping you achieve your future sustainability goals.

Our soon-to-be-released DAG-based architecture will seamlessly integrate with your existing systems, delivering real-time insights into your supply chain operations. We’re also introducing a token-based rewards system to encourage sustainable practices, actively involving your customers and partners in circular economy initiatives. With OpenChain’s forthcoming DAG-based solution, you’ll be well-positioned to meet global regulatory standards while advancing your commitment to a more sustainable future.
